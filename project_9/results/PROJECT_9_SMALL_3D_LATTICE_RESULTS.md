# PROJECT 9 SMALL 3D LATTICE RESULTS
## First Higher-Dimensional Compositional Sandbox Result

**Date:** April 2026  
**Project:** 9  
**Status:** COMPLETE  
**Verdict:** PASS

---

## Purpose

This document records the first accepted result in Project 9.

Its purpose was to test whether a minimal 3D arithmetic-like compositional sandbox can exhibit genuine local-to-global effects.

---

## Main Result

The first 3D sandbox produced a clear nontrivial effect:

- a perturbation at a single local cell
- changed multiple other cells in the lattice
- while leaving the total carry count unchanged

This is exactly the kind of first signal Project 9 needed.

---

## What This Establishes

This result establishes the following:

1. the 3D sandbox is not trivial
2. local perturbations can propagate beyond a single cell
3. the system exhibits genuine local-to-global sensitivity
4. the effect is structural rather than merely a simple scalar collapse

This is a valid first compositional result.

---

## Why This Matters

Project 9 was introduced to ask whether the local-to-global issues seen in the earlier linear-sequence projects persist or change in a higher-dimensional structured state space.

This result shows that the sandbox is rich enough to support that question.

Without this result, Project 9 would remain only a conceptual proposal.
With it, the project now has a real experimental foundation.

---

## Correct Interpretation

The strongest safe interpretation is:

> the first higher-dimensional arithmetic-like sandbox already exhibits meaningful local-to-global propagation, making it suitable for further composition and intervention research.

This is a strong first result for Project 9.

---

## What This Does NOT Yet Prove

This result does not yet prove:

- that the 3D sandbox is the best sandbox design
- that the observed propagation pattern is already well understood
- that higher-dimensional composition behaves fundamentally better or worse than linear composition
- or that Project 9 has reached its core scientific result

It only proves that:
> the sandbox is scientifically alive.

---

## Why This Is a Good Start

This result is important because it gives Project 9 exactly what a new branch needs:

- a real signal
- a nontrivial state space
- a clear local-to-global effect
- and a reason to continue

That is enough to justify the next experiments.

---

## Recommended Next Step

A natural next step is now:

- compare multiple perturbation locations
- compare perturbation magnitudes
- test whether different regions of the 3D lattice have different propagation profiles
- and ask whether family-like behavior can be defined inside this higher-dimensional space
