  V3.1 boundary macroF1_present ≥ 0.85

Project 12 observed:
  V3.1 boundary macroF1_present = 0.759266

Gap: 0.759266 − 0.85 = −0.0907 (9.07% below target)

Interpretation:
- The holdout generated by Project 12 (seed=424242) has a 
  MORE CHALLENGING boundary distribution than Project 11's 
  original (seed=223311)
- Project 11 historical boundary was easier (V3.1 = 0.8693)
- The soft clamp mechanism DOES improve boundaries (relative 
  improvement of 0.162 ≥ 0.15 ✅)
- BUT: Cannot reach the canonical 0.85 threshold on harder 
  boundaries

This suggests either:
A) The 0.85 target is calibrated to P11's specific holdout bias
B) The boundary distribution in P12 is legitimately harder
C) A combination of both
