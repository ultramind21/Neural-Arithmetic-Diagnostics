## 2. Starting Point of Phase 2

Phase 2 began with the revised higher-order candidate:

## 3. What Phase 2 Found

Phase 2 produced three major theoretical findings:

### A. Narrow support region
The revised higher-order candidate held only in a narrow portion of the tested regime space.

### B. Regime-space dependence
Comparative rescue behavior depended strongly on regime position rather than on one stable higher-order necessity rule.

### C. Threshold structure
The strongest current evidence suggests threshold-like transition regions in:
- heterogeneity
- and universal rescue power

These three findings define the current Phase 2 contribution.
