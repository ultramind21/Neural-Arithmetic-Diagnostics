## Overview

This repository documents a full research line on **arithmetic reasoning in neural networks**.

It contains three major layers:

1. **Projects 1–3** — the original research line and its major findings  
2. **Trust-Recovery Audit** — a structured verification archive that revalidated, qualified, or bounded earlier results  
3. **Project 4** — a post-audit diagnostic framework project focused on distinguishing narrow gains from broader structural robustness

The repository is therefore not just a collection of experiments.  
It is:
- a research program,
- an audited archive,
- and a post-audit methodological extension.

## Most Important Repository-Wide Position

The repository now supports the following broad position:

> High arithmetic accuracy alone is not sufficient evidence of robust reasoning.
> Structured diagnostics, explicit validation, and careful qualification are necessary to distinguish apparent success from more meaningful robustness.

This is the unifying lesson that links:
- Projects 1–3
- the audit
- and Project 4
