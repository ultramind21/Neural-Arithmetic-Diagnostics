## 2. Current Accepted Result Chain

### Result 1
**The first 3D arithmetic-like sandbox is nontrivial.**

A single local perturbation can affect multiple other cells, which establishes real local-to-global propagation.

### Result 2
**Scaling the lattice breaks perturbation symmetry.**

Moving from `2×2×2` to `3×2×2` reveals topology-sensitive differentiation:
- edge-like positions have stronger perturbation reach than corner-like positions.

### Result 3
**Family identity shapes propagation.**

The sandbox does not only reflect topology.
It also reflects family-sensitive higher-dimensional structure.

### Result 4
**Rescue behavior is family-sensitive.**

Same-family and cross-family rescue do not behave equivalently.
Cross-family rescue can increase disruption.

### Result 5
**Adaptive family-aware rescue works better than naive rescue logic.**

The system can preserve the best currently available rescue behavior while avoiding the harmful interference caused by non-adaptive rescue.
