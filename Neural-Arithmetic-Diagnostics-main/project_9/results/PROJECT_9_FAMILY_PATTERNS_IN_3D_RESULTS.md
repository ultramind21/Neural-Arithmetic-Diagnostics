## Main Result

The experiment showed that family membership affects perturbation propagation.

In the `3×2×2` sandbox:
- the transition family exhibited higher perturbation reach
- the low and high families exhibited lower perturbation reach
- carry-dynamic responses also differed across family groupings

This is the first result showing that the 3D sandbox is not only topology-sensitive, but also family-sensitive.
